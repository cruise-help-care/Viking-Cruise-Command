Cancellation Policy Summary

  • Cancel 75+ days before for short cruises (1–4 nights) for refund (excl. deposit).
  • Cancel 90+ days before for cruises 5 nights or longer.
  • Use Cruise with Confidence: cancel up to 48 hrs before for Future Cruise Credit.
